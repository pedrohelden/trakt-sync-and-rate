Trakt Sync & Rate

Features to Enable

When enabled, your existing ratings will be shown as stream options in Stremio. Clicking on a current rating will remove it (requires "Remove Rating" option enabled).
When enabled, a separate "Remove Rating" option will appear alongside current ratings. This allows you to easily remove ratings you've previously set.
When enabled, older watched entries for the same movie/episode will be automatically deleted when:
1. Marking as watched directly
2. Rating with "Automatically mark as played when rating" enabled
This helps maintain a clean Trakt history.
NEW Current Rating Feature: When you have already rated a movie or episode, it will appear as a stream option. Clicking it will remove your rating if "Remove Rating" is enabled.

Stream Ordering (Drag & Drop)

Drag the handles to reorder how the options appear in Stremio. The order you set here will be reflected in the stream list.
